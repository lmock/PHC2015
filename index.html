Torree McGowan

Monica McLemore
Asst. Adjunct Professor
UCSF School of Nursing

Ulrike Muench
Asst. Professor
UCSF School of Nursing

Ulrike Muench, PhD, RN is an interdisciplinary health services researcher at the intersection of nursing, health policy, medical sociology, and healthcare economics.

Sophie Park
Director of Operations & Marketing
Health 2.0

Sophie Park is currently the Director of Operations & Marketing at Health 2.0. She decided to take a hiatus from academia and jump right into becoming a part of the digital health technology industry.

Ken Tao
Program Director
Pre-Medical Surgical Internship Program

Ken Tao was a participant of the UC Davis Pre-Medical Surgical Internship in the Summer of 2012. Because he thoroughly enjoyed the program, he decided to continue as a program director to help run the program.

Flora Ting
MCAT/DAT/OAT Instructor
Kaplan Test Prep Inc.

Since receiving her Bachelor's degree in Molecular and Cell Biology in 2013, Flora has worked with students to build their strengths and prepare for MCAT, DAT, and OAT exams while simultaneously working in a cancer research lab in Oakland.

Jeffrey Weitzman
Emergency & Internal Medicine
Marin General Hospital

Jeffrey Weitzman, MD is an Emergency Physician at Marin General Hospital. He also practices in the Urgent Care clinic at UC Berkeley student health in the Tang Center, and at the Tamalpais Senior facility in Marin County providing Internal Medicine and Geriatric medical care.

Noriko Yoshikawa
Otolaryngology
Kaiser Permanente

Noriko Yoshikawa, MD is an otolaryngologist at Kaiser Permanente. She loves this specialty as it allows her to treat a wide range of patients in terms of age and demographics.
